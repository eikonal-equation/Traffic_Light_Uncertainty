Optimal Driving Examples

These examples are from the following paper:

View manuscript on Arxiv »

All of the computations use the following parameters:

  • Max allowed distance: \(\bar{d} = 100\) m
  • Traffic light location: \(d_{\ell} = 0\) m
  • Target location: \(d^* = -100\) m
  • Max allowed velocity: \(\bar{v} = 45\) mph \(\approx 20.12 m/s\)
  • Min allowed velocity: \(v = 0\) mph
  • Min acceleration: \(-\alpha = -3.8\) m/s/s
  • Max acceleration: \(\beta = +3.8\) m/s/s
  • Yellow light duration: \(D_Y = 3\) s
  • Red light duration: \(D_R = 60\) s
We conduct all of our examples / experiments on a \(345 \times 181\) grid in \((d,v)\) space.
